    RAPTURE Backtop¶

    The RAPTURE Backtop is a proprietary Backpack PC / Haptic Suit combo created by The VOID in collaboration with Optimal Design (now owned by Deloitte).

    Variants¶

    There are two variants of RAPTURE Backtop that were used in VOID Terminals.

    "Flat Top" Not much is known about "Flat Top" Backtops. From what is known, these were used up until somewhere between mid 2018 and mid 2019.
