# Hardware

This section documents hardware and props used inside The VOID, including proprietary and off-the-shelf devices.  

This doc is an attempt to condense *years* worth of knowledge strewn across various minds and Discord servers. What you see may be subject to change.

!!! info "Disclaimer"
